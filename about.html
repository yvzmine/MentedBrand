Our Story

Created in 2017 by Co-Founders KJ Miller and Amanda E. Johnson, Mented exists to celebrate all hues and to make beauty truly, wonderfully inclusive. We created Mented Cosmetics because we believe everyone should be able to find themselves in the world of beauty, no matter your skin tone. We know you'll love being put first - because when it comes to beauty, no one deserves to be an afterthought.

KJ Miller

CEO and Co-Founder
